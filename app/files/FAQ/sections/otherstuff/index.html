How do I get my child into the Small World Daycare?

Small World takes infants at 8 weeks to children age 12, and has a strong educational and active program to help children learn and stay school ready! To place your child in the Small World Daycare, please call the college and ask to talk to Small World about placement availability. It is recommended to do this is advance as much as possible, so that accommodations in classrooms can be made. If you have an emergency daycare need that affects your ability to go to WVC classes, please contact the Retention Coordinator for assistance. Small World does take Project Child as a payment option and has packets available for you to submit to Project Child to get qualified.

Where can I get free software for my computer?

YES! As a WVC student, staff or faculty member with an iecc.edu email, you are eligible to sign up for Free Office 365, a cloud based software package that provides the most commonly used office software. You can learn how to sign up by logging into your Entrata account and clicking on the “Software” tab on the left side of the screen.

What is the 1098t tax form for?

The 1098t tax form is available to you for submission as a deduction on your income tax when you file. We recommend you contact your tax preparer or refer to www.irs.gov to find out additional information on how this tax form may be of use to you. If you need to access your form, you can find that documentation on your Entrata account by clicking on the “STUDENTS” tab on the left and then looking on the right hand side under “Taxes-1098t.”
